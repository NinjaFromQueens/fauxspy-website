The signs you're looking at a catfish

Before you even check if a photo is fake, watch for these red flags. They often show up together on catfish profiles.

  • Too perfect: The photos are all glamorous, professional, or model-quality. Real people have bad lighting selfies. Catfishers don't.
  • Limited photos: Only one or two main shots, no casual pictures, no group photos. AI generators usually make one image at a time.
  • Won't video chat: They have excuses—bad camera, poor connection, about to go on a trip. Real people eventually get on video.
  • Inconsistent stories: Their job changes. Their location shifts. Their family situation doesn't add up. Liars contradict themselves.
  • Fast escalation: They're "falling for you" within days. They talk about meeting but never actually plan it. They ask for money or personal info.
  • Weird facial features: In AI images, look for asymmetrical ears, odd tooth patterns, unnatural skin blending, or eyes that don't quite match.
