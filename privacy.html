Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

What We Collect

Faux Spy is built with privacy in mind. We collect the absolute minimum data needed to provide our service:

  • Image URLs — Sent to our detection API only when you investigate an image. We don't store these.
  • Email address — Only if you subscribe to a paid plan. Used for license delivery and support.
  • Usage statistics — Stored locally in your browser. Never sent to us.

What We Don't Collect

  • ❌ Your browsing history
  • ❌ Personal identifiable information
  • ❌ Cookies or tracking pixels
  • ❌ Images themselves (only the URL)
  • ❌ Detection results history (stays in your browser)

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to provide Faux Spy:

  • Hive AI — Image detection. Receives image URLs only.
  • Stripe — Payment processing for paid plans.
  • Vercel — Hosting our website and API.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Cancel your subscription anytime from your account
  • Request deletion of your email and license records
  • Export your local stats (from extension settings)
