What Faux Spy detects (and what it means for POF)

No AI Detected. The photo is real. It passed the test. Doesn't mean the person is trustworthy—that's on you—but the image is genuine.

AI Photo. The entire image was generated by AI (DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion). This person doesn't exist. They're a scammer or bot. Unmatch immediately.

Deepfake. A real face was swapped or manipulated onto another body or expression. The person exists, but the photo is fake. Common in romance scams. Unmatch.

Digital Art or Possible Manipulation. The image has been edited or drawn. Could be a filter, could be fraud. Use judgment.

Inconclusive. The AI can't decide. This doesn't mean the photo is real—it means you should ask for a video call before meeting.
