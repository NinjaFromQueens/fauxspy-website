What Faux Spy actually detects

This isn't a beauty filter or mood detector. Faux Spy analyzes images for signs of artificial generation or manipulation:

  • AI Photo: Generated by DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or other AI art generators. Looks realistic but was never a real person.
  • AI Art: Stylized AI output. Less photo-realistic, more creative—but still not a real picture.
  • Digital Art: Hand-created artwork or heavily stylized images. Not AI-generated, but not a real photo either.
  • Possible Manipulation: Edited, filtered, or altered. The person in the photo might be real, but the image has been changed significantly.
  • No AI Detected: The image appears to be an unmanipulated photograph. Most likely a real person.
  • Inconclusive: Not enough data to determine. Try a different angle or photo.

Each result includes a confidence score. An 89% confidence that the image is AI-generated is worth taking seriously. A 51% inconclusive result means try another photo from that profile.
