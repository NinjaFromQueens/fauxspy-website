What Faux Spy detects on Raya

Faux Spy analyzes every photo at the pixel level. When you hover over or right-click a Raya profile image, the extension runs detection and returns one of six verdicts:

  • No AI Detected: The photo passed all checks. Real image.
  • AI Photo: Generated entirely by AI. Deepfake or synthetic image.
  • AI Art: Stylized or artistic AI image. Less common in dating profiles, but worth knowing.
  • Digital Art: Edited or drawn by human hands. Not a real photo.
  • Possible Manipulation: The photo has signs of editing—filters, face-tuning, background alterations. Real person, but altered.
  • Inconclusive: The image is ambiguous. Not enough data to call it. Request more photos from the profile.

Each verdict comes with a confidence score. 95% confidence means you can trust it. 60% confidence means you need a second opinion—check another photo.
