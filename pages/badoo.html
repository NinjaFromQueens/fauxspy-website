What Faux Spy catches on Badoo

AI Photo: Generated by DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or similar. Common in catfish scams. The profile doesn't belong to a real person.

Real Photo: Unaltered photograph of an actual person. Safe to proceed (though always use basic due diligence—real photos can still belong to scammers using stolen identities).

Digital Art: Illustrated or digitally painted. Sometimes intentional (artists on dating apps), sometimes a red flag.

Possible Manipulation: A real photo that's been edited or altered in ways that change the person's appearance. Common in catfish profiles.

Inconclusive: The model isn't sure. Happens with older photos, heavy filters, or unusual angles. When in doubt, trust your instinct and ask for a video call.
