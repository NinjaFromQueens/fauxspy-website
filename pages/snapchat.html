The difference between real profiles and AI-generated catfish

Real Snapchat photos have consistent lighting, natural skin texture, and slight imperfections. AI-generated images often show symmetrical faces, smooth skin, slightly blurred backgrounds, and subtle artifacts around the ears, teeth, or hair edges.

But don't rely on eyeballing it. AI image quality has improved to the point where human inspection is unreliable. That's why Faux Spy analyzes pixel-level patterns instead of asking you to guess. You get one of six verdicts: No AI Detected, AI Photo, AI Art, Digital Art, Possible Manipulation, or Inconclusive.

Snapchat's AI filters and native editing tools complicate things further. Someone could use a legitimate Snapchat filter, which Faux Spy might flag as "Digital Art." But that's fine—it tells you the image has been altered, which is useful context when deciding whether to trust a profile.
