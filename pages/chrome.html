Why you need an AI image detector in Chrome

AI-generated profile photos are now indistinguishable from real ones to the human eye. A 2025 Microsoft study found people correctly identify AI images only 61% of the time — barely better than guessing. The problem: modern AI generators create faces that have never existed, so reverse image search finds nothing. There's no original photo to match against.

Romance scam victims in the US lost $1.14 billion in 2023 (FTC). The entry point is almost always a convincing fake profile photo. AI generators like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, and Stable Diffusion produce these at scale — a scammer can generate 100 unique fake identities in minutes.

Faux Spy uses machine learning pixel analysis to find what human eyes miss. AI generators leave subtle artifacts in image noise, frequency patterns, and lighting behavior. The detector finds these in seconds, on any image, right inside Chrome.
