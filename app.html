How Faux Spy Works on Your Phone

No switching apps. No copying links. Just share and get your answer — works anywhere you can browse photos on your iPhone or Android.

📱

See a suspicious photo

Browsing a dating profile, social media feed, or news article and something looks a little too perfect? A little too smooth? That's when you need Faux Spy.

Tap share → choose Faux Spy

Tap the share button on the photo. Faux Spy appears in your share sheet — right next to AirDrop and Messages. Tap it. That's it. Works from Safari, your camera roll, and any app that supports sharing.

An AI probability score in seconds. Faux Spy flags GAN face synthesis, missing camera data, AI-typical dimensions, and more. No guessing. Just a clear verdict you can trust.
